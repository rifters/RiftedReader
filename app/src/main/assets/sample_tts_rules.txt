# Sample TTS replacement rules adapted from LibreraReader (docs/faq/tts-replacements)
"Dr." "Doctor"
"Mr." "Mister"
"Mrs." "Misses"
"Ms." "Miss"
"Jr." "Junior"
"Sr." "Senior"
"PDF" "P D F"
"HTML" "H T M L"
"URL" "U R L"

# Replace dash-like punctuation with a short pause
"*[?!:;–—―]" ","

# Remove bracketed footnotes
"*\[(\d+)\]" "ttsSKIP"

# Skip page labels
"Page \d+" "ttsSKIP"

# Stop when section end markers appear
"End of Chapter" "ttsSTOP"
